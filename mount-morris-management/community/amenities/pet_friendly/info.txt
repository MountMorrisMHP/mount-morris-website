Display Name: Pet Friendly
Order: 4
Description: One domesticated pet per household is welcome with written approval from the office.
Pet Rent: $25 per month.
Probation: Approved pets have a 90-day probation period.
Registration: Pets must be registered with the office, with proof of rabies vaccination and any shots required by law; a current rabies tag must be worn if required.
Leash: Pets must be on a leash any time they are outside your home or off your site.
Cleanup: Owners must clean up after their pets daily, on the site and throughout the park.
Note: Service animals under Michigan law are not considered pets. Fenced enclosures, dog houses, and "Beware of Dog" signs are not permitted.
