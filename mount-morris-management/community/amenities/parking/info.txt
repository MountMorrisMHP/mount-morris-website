Display Name: Parking
Order: 8
Description: Designated parking is provided throughout the community.
Spaces: Park only in designated spaces, not on lawns, streets, vacant sites, or patios. Double parking is not permitted.
Vehicle Size: Vehicles with a load capacity over three-quarter ton are not kept or stored in the park.
Speed & Signs: Posted speed limits and traffic signs must be obeyed. No parking within 10 feet of fire hydrants.
