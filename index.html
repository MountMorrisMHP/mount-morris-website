Good to know

Questions we hear a lot

Our community guidelines keep the neighborhood safe, quiet, and well-kept for everyone. You'll get a full copy of the rules during your tour, covering upkeep, parking, guests, and quiet hours.

Alongside your home payment or rent, there's a monthly lot rent that covers your homesite and shared amenities. We'll walk you through every cost up front — no surprises.

Each home has its own meter, so you only pay for the water you use. Readings are simple and billing is clearly itemized each month.

We're a pet-friendly community and love four-legged neighbors. A few breed and size guidelines apply, and there's plenty of green space for walks.
